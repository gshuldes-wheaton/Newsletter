RENOVATIONS!!

We would be remiss to begin anywhere but with the completion of our renovations! Beginning at the end of the last Academic Year and stretching into mid-July, Balfour Natatorium underwent some incredibly exciting renovations including, but not limited to; refurnished walls and benches, thorough pool cleaning, thorough deck and tile cleaning, and perhaps the most exciting parts - water agitators for diving, and a brand new bulkhead! Over the course of the next year, more renovations will be completed to continue to improve the overall health of our pool, as well as increase the standard of quality for those that use it!

FALL FEST AND ALUMNI MEET

We are happy to introduce and announce the return of the Alumni Meet this year as a part of the Wheaton College Fall Fest! Our team as it is now is a culmination of all of the athletes that competed as a Lyon before - and we want to see you in the pool again! On October 4th, at 11am, we will be hosting our Alumni Meet with a reception to be held afterwards.

 

ALUMNI SPOTLIGHT

We want to hear from you! Let us know about anyone in the Wheaton Family that are making waves in their life outside of Wheaton, or are doing something that you feel deserves to be recognized!
